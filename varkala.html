Varkala

Where Cliffs Meet the Arabian Sea

About Varkala

Varkala is a stunning coastal town in Kerala's Thiruvananthapuram district, famous for its dramatic red cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea. It is one of the few places in India where cliffs rise directly beside the beach, creating breathtaking views and unforgettable sunsets.

Varkala is a perfect destination for beach lovers, backpackers, surfers, yoga enthusiasts, and couples seeking a peaceful getaway. The town is also known for its spiritual significance, Ayurvedic wellness centers, and lively cliffside cafés.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Varkala is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant for beach activities, sightseeing, and enjoying the vibrant atmosphere along the North Cliff.

Top Attractions

  • Varkala Cliff
  • Papanasam Beach
  • Black Sand Beach
  • Janardhana Swamy Temple
  • Kappil Beach
  • Kappil Lake
  • Anjengo Fort
  • Varkala Aquarium

Things to Do

  • Relax on the beautiful beaches.
  • Watch spectacular sunsets from the cliff.
  • Enjoy surfing and parasailing.
  • Take yoga and meditation classes.
  • Experience Ayurvedic spa treatments.
  • Shop for handmade souvenirs and local crafts.

Must-Try Foods

  • Grilled Seafood
  • Karimeen Pollichathu
  • Appam with Stew
  • Prawn Curry
  • Kerala Sadya
  • Fresh Tropical Fruit Juices

Travel Tips

  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat.
  • Wear comfortable footwear for walking along the cliffs.
  • Visit the beach during sunset for the best views.
  • Follow safety instructions while swimming.
  • Keep the beach clean and avoid plastic waste.
