Munnar

The Land of Endless Tea Gardens

About Munnar

Munnar is one of Kerala's most famous hill stations, located about 1,600 meters above sea level in the Western Ghats. It is known for its vast tea plantations, cool climate, mist-covered mountains, waterfalls, and peaceful atmosphere. Every year, thousands of tourists visit Munnar to enjoy nature, trekking, wildlife, and breathtaking landscapes.

Best Time to Visit

October to March offers pleasant weather for sightseeing and outdoor activities. During the monsoon season (June–September), Munnar becomes lush green with beautiful waterfalls.

Top Attractions

  • Eravikulam National Park
  • Tea Museum
  • Mattupetty Dam
  • Echo Point
  • Top Station
  • Attukad Waterfalls
  • Blossom Park

Things To Do

  • Tea plantation walk
  • Trekking
  • Photography
  • Boating
  • Wildlife safari
  • Camping

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm clothes.
  • Wear comfortable trekking shoes.
  • Book hotels early during peak season.
  • Start sightseeing early in the morning.
