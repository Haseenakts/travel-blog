Kovalam

Kerala's Beach Paradise

About Kovalam

Kovalam is one of Kerala's most famous beach destinations, located near Thiruvananthapuram. Known for its crescent-shaped beaches, golden sands, swaying coconut palms, and stunning Arabian Sea views, Kovalam attracts visitors from around the world.

The destination is perfect for beach lovers, honeymooners, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to relax by the sea. Kovalam is also renowned for Ayurvedic wellness treatments and vibrant seaside cafés.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kovalam is from October to March. During this period, the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities, sightseeing, and water sports.

Top Attractions

  • Lighthouse Beach
  • Hawa Beach
  • Samudra Beach
  • Kovalam Lighthouse
  • Vizhinjam Fishing Harbour
  • Halcyon Castle
  • Ayurvedic Wellness Centers
  • Nearby Poovar Island

Things to Do

  • Relax on the beautiful beaches.
  • Watch breathtaking sunsets over the Arabian Sea.
  • Try surfing and other water sports.
  • Climb the lighthouse for panoramic views.
  • Enjoy Ayurvedic spa and wellness treatments.
  • Taste fresh seafood at beachside restaurants.

Must-Try Foods

  • Karimeen Pollichathu
  • Kerala Fish Curry
  • Prawn Roast
  • Appam and Stew
  • Malabar Parotta with Curry
  • Fresh Coconut Water

Travel Tips

  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat.
  • Stay hydrated during beach activities.
  • Visit the lighthouse during sunset hours.
  • Wear comfortable beach footwear.
  • Respect local customs and keep beaches clean.
