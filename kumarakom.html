Kumarakom

A Peaceful Backwater Paradise

About Kumarakom

Kumarakom is a charming village on the banks of the beautiful Vembanad Lake in Kottayam district. Famous for its serene backwaters, luxury houseboats, bird sanctuary, and lush green landscapes, Kumarakom is one of Kerala's most relaxing destinations.

Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a peaceful retreat, Kumarakom offers an unforgettable experience surrounded by nature and traditional Kerala culture.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kumarakom is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant for houseboat cruises, birdwatching, and sightseeing.

Top Attractions

  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Vembanad Lake
  • Houseboat Cruise
  • Pathiramanal Island
  • Bay Island Driftwood Museum
  • Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls
  • St. Mary's Church
  • Luxury Backwater Resorts

Things to Do

  • Enjoy an overnight houseboat stay.
  • Go birdwatching at Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary.
  • Take a sunset cruise on Vembanad Lake.
  • Experience authentic village life with canoe rides.
  • Relax with Ayurvedic spa treatments.
  • Capture stunning backwater landscapes.

Must-Try Foods

  • Karimeen Pollichathu (Pearl Spot Fish)
  • Kerala Fish Curry
  • Appam with Duck Roast
  • Prawn Roast
  • Kerala Sadya
  • Fresh Coconut Water

Travel Tips

  • Book your houseboat early during peak season.
  • Carry light cotton clothes and sunscreen.
  • Visit the bird sanctuary early in the morning.
  • Don't forget your camera for beautiful sunrise and sunset views.
  • Respect nature and avoid littering.
