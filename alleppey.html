Alleppey

The Venice of the East

About Alleppey

Alleppey (Alappuzha) is one of Kerala's most famous tourist destinations, renowned for its tranquil backwaters, picturesque canals, lush paddy fields, and traditional houseboats. Often called the "Venice of the East", Alleppey offers visitors a unique opportunity to experience Kerala's natural beauty and village life while cruising through its serene waterways.

Whether you're looking for a relaxing houseboat stay, authentic Kerala cuisine, or peaceful sunset views, Alleppey provides an unforgettable travel experience.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Alleppey is from October to March, when the weather is cool and pleasant for sightseeing and houseboat cruises. The monsoon season (June to September) transforms the backwaters into lush green landscapes, making it equally beautiful for nature lovers.

Top Attractions

  • Alleppey Backwaters
  • Luxury Houseboat Cruise
  • Alappuzha Beach
  • Marari Beach
  • Pathiramanal Island
  • Krishnapuram Palace
  • Ambalappuzha Sri Krishna Temple
  • Vembanad Lake

Things to Do

  • Stay overnight in a traditional houseboat.
  • Watch a beautiful sunset over Vembanad Lake.
  • Taste authentic Kerala seafood and Sadya.
  • Enjoy canoe rides through narrow canals.
  • Visit local coir-making villages.
  • Experience the famous Nehru Trophy Boat Race (August).

Must-Try Foods

  • Karimeen Pollichathu (Pearl Spot Fish)
  • Kerala Fish Curry
  • Prawn Roast
  • Appam with Stew
  • Kerala Sadya
  • Payasam

Travel Tips

  • Book your houseboat in advance during peak season.
  • Carry light cotton clothes and sunscreen.
  • Start your backwater cruise early in the morning.
  • Keep your camera ready for beautiful sunsets and birdlife.
  • Respect the local environment and avoid littering.
