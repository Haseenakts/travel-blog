Bekal Fort

Kerala's Majestic Seaside Fortress

About Bekal Fort

Bekal Fort, located in Kasaragod district, is the largest and one of the best-preserved forts in Kerala. Built in the 17th century, this magnificent fort overlooks the Arabian Sea and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the coastline. Its impressive architecture, historic significance, and scenic surroundings make it one of Kerala's most popular tourist attractions.

The fort has been featured in several Indian films and is a perfect destination for history enthusiasts, photographers, and families looking to enjoy a relaxing day by the sea.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Bekal Fort is from October to March, when the weather is cool and pleasant for exploring the fort, nearby beaches, and gardens.

Top Attractions

  • Bekal Fort
  • Bekal Beach
  • Observation Tower
  • Sea View Walkway
  • Pallikere Beach
  • Kappil Beach
  • Bekal Hole Aqua Park
  • Chandragiri Fort

Things to Do

  • Explore the historic Bekal Fort.
  • Walk along the scenic seaside pathways.
  • Enjoy stunning sunset views over the Arabian Sea.
  • Relax at Bekal and Pallikere beaches.
  • Capture panoramic photographs from the observation tower.
  • Visit nearby parks and gardens.

Must-Try Foods

  • Malabar Biryani
  • Fish Curry with Kerala Rice
  • Pathiri with Chicken Curry
  • Seafood Platter
  • Kerala Sadya
  • Unnakkaya and Kozhikodan Halwa

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for exploring the fort.
  • Carry water, sunscreen, and a hat during sunny days.
  • Visit in the evening for beautiful sunset views.
  • Keep your camera ready for spectacular coastal scenery.
  • Help preserve the heritage site by avoiding littering.
