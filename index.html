About Kerala

Kerala, popularly known as "God's Own Country", is one of India's most beautiful travel destinations. From misty hill stations and tranquil backwaters to golden beaches, waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries, and vibrant culture, Kerala offers unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Travel Tips

🌴 Best Time

October to March

🍛 Food

Try Sadya, Appam, Fish Curry & Payasam.

🚗 Transport

Rent a car or use KSRTC buses for sightseeing.

📷 Don't Miss

Sunrise, tea gardens and houseboat cruises.
