Poovar

Where the River Meets the Sea

About Poovar

Poovar is a beautiful coastal village located about 30 km from Thiruvananthapuram. It is famous for its unique meeting point of the Neyyar River, the Arabian Sea, and the tranquil backwaters. Surrounded by golden sand beaches, coconut groves, mangrove forests, and floating resorts, Poovar is one of Kerala's most peaceful and picturesque destinations.

Whether you're seeking relaxation, nature, or a romantic getaway, Poovar offers unforgettable experiences with its calm waters, scenic landscapes, and traditional Kerala hospitality.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Poovar is from October to March, when the weather is cool and ideal for boating, sightseeing, and enjoying the beaches.

Top Attractions

  • Poovar Backwaters
  • Golden Sand Beach
  • Floating Cottages
  • Mangrove Forests
  • Neyyar River
  • Boat Cruises
  • Poovar Island
  • Nearby Kovalam Beach

Things to Do

  • Enjoy a peaceful backwater boat ride.
  • Stay in a floating resort.
  • Relax on the golden sand beach.
  • Watch spectacular sunsets over the Arabian Sea.
  • Explore the mangrove forests by boat.
  • Capture beautiful nature and bird photography.

Must-Try Foods

  • Karimeen Pollichathu
  • Kerala Fish Curry
  • Prawn Roast
  • Appam with Stew
  • Kerala Sadya
  • Fresh Coconut Water

Travel Tips

  • Book boat rides and floating cottages in advance.
  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat.
  • Visit during sunrise or sunset for the best views.
  • Wear comfortable clothing suitable for boating.
  • Help keep the backwaters clean by avoiding plastic waste.
